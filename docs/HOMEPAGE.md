# IGLOUE Homepage Plan

## Purpose

The homepage should help a visitor quickly decide whether they can rent a portable air conditioner from IGLOUE.

The homepage is not a brochure.

It is the start of the booking journey.

---

## Primary Customer Problem

The visitor is hot, uncomfortable, or preparing for hot weather.

They want to know:

- Can IGLOUE deliver to me?
- How much will it cost?
- What do I get?
- How quickly can I book?
- Can I trust this company?

---

## Homepage Journey

### 1. Hero

Question:

Can IGLOUE help me?

Answer:

Portable air conditioner rental in Charente.

Main action:

Check my postcode.

---

### 2. Postcode Checker

Question:

Can you deliver to my address?

Answer:

Show delivery zone, delivery price and first-week estimate.

---

### 3. Rental Offer

Question:

What am I renting?

Answer:

A portable air conditioner from 19 € per week.

---

### 4. Setup Option

Question:

Do I need help installing it?

Answer:

Optional mise en service for 9,99 €.

Includes:

- placing the unit in the room
- plugging it in
- fitting the window outlet kit
- explaining basic use

---

### 5. Delivery Explanation

Question:

What does the delivery price include?

Answer:

One-off round-trip delivery and collection.

---

### 6. Trust

Question:

Can I trust IGLOUE?

Answer:

Transparent prices, local Charente service, simple process, clear communication.

---

### 7. Booking

Question:

What do I do next?

Answer:

Choose rental dates and send a booking request.

---

## Homepage Rule

Every section must reduce uncertainty.

If a section does not help the customer decide, remove it.
