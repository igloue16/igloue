# IGLOUE Brand Guidelines

## Brand Purpose

IGLOUE exists to make staying cool during hot weather simple, affordable and stress-free.

Customers should never feel confused about pricing, delivery or booking.

Everything should feel transparent, modern and trustworthy.

---

## Brand Personality

IGLOUE is:

• Premium
• Calm
• Modern
• Honest
• Friendly
• Professional
• Reliable
• Local

IGLOUE is never:

• Loud
• Cheap-looking
• Flashy
• Complicated
• Pushy
• Confusing

---

## Brand Promise

Simple pricing.

Simple booking.

Simple delivery.

Simple comfort.

---

## Customer Emotion

When someone lands on the website they should immediately feel:

"I've found the right company."

Not:

"I need to compare ten more websites."

---

## Target Customer

Primary

• Homeowners
• Renters
• Families
• Elderly people
• Home workers

Secondary

• Small businesses
• Offices
• Holiday rentals
• Events

---

## Core Values

Transparency

Reliability

Comfort

Professionalism

Simplicity

Local service

---

## Writing Style

Short sentences.

Simple words.

No technical jargon.

No marketing fluff.

Explain everything clearly.

---

## Visual Style

Large white space.

Rounded corners.

Cool colours.

Soft shadows.

Large photography.

Friendly icons.

Premium spacing.

---

## Photography Style

Bright homes.

Natural light.

Real products.

Real people.

Summer atmosphere.

Comfort.

Never fake-looking stock images.

---

## Website Goals

Answer the customer's questions as quickly as possible.

Remove uncertainty.

Encourage booking.

Build trust.

Everything else is secondary.
