# Feature 001 — Rental Assistant

## Objective

Help a customer quickly understand whether IGLOUE can deliver to them, what it will cost, and which rental option fits their need before asking for personal details.

---

## Current Scope

French only.

Portable air conditioner rental only.

Charente department 16 only.

No payments yet.

No customer account yet.

No confirmed booking yet.

---

## Customer Journey

1. Enter postcode
2. Show delivery availability
3. Show delivery zone
4. Show one-off delivery and collection price
5. Select room type
6. Select approximate room size
7. Recommend a portable AC option
8. Choose optional mise en service
9. Show estimated first-week total
10. Continue to booking request

---

## Inputs

- Postcode
- Room type
- Room size
- Setup option

---

## Outputs

- Delivery available or unavailable
- Delivery zone
- Delivery and collection price
- Recommended unit
- Weekly rental price
- Setup price
- Estimated first-week total

---

## Pricing Rules

Weekly
